# Baseroot V2 Whitepaper
## Decentralized Knowledge Liquidity & AI Agent Commerce Protocol

### 1. Abstract
Baseroot V2 is a decentralized protocol that enables AI agents to access DAO-owned verified datasets under programmable licenses, while automatically distributing revenue to data owners based on actual usage. The protocol introduces a new economic layer where knowledge becomes a yield-generating digital asset.

### 2. Problem Statement
High-value research and domain-specific datasets are produced by DAOs, DeSci communities, and research collectives. However, current AI ecosystems extract value from these datasets without fair attribution, transparency, or sustainable revenue models for the original data producers.

### 3. Solution Overview
Baseroot V2 extends the AI agent marketplace model by introducing Verified Data Pools and a trustless revenue routing mechanism. AI agents remain developer-hosted, while datasets are registered with cryptographic proofs, licenses, and royalty policies enforced by smart contracts.

### 4. System Architecture
The architecture consists of on-chain registries for agents and datasets, a revenue router smart contract for automated payouts, and off-chain services for authentication, usage verification, and analytics. Raw data remains off-chain and encrypted.

### 5. Verified Data Pool Model
Datasets are registered by DAO-controlled wallets with immutable hashes and programmable licenses. AI agents never directly download data; instead, computation is performed in controlled environments, ensuring data sovereignty and preventing leakage.

### 6. AI Agent Integration
Developers deploy and maintain their own AI agents. Each agent is registered with the protocol and receives an Agent ID. All inference requests require signed authorization tokens, and any dataset usage must be reported via usage attestations.

### 7. Economic Model
Payments are triggered by successful inference executions with verified dataset usage. Revenue is automatically split between the dataset-owning DAO, the AI agent developer, and the Baseroot protocol according to predefined policies.

### 8. Marketplace & User Experience
Users interact with a transparent marketplace where AI agents are listed alongside their verified data sources. Users purchase outcomes and insights rather than opaque AI responses, increasing trust and accountability.

### 9. Governance & Legal Positioning
The protocol employs multi-layer governance, separating protocol-level rules from DAO-level data ownership. Baseroot operates as a neutral intermediary protocol, minimizing legal exposure related to data ownership and licensing.

### 10. MVP Roadmap
The initial MVP focuses on a minimal but complete economic loop: one DAO, one dataset, one AI agent, and real on-chain revenue distribution within 90 days.

### 11. Long-Term Vision
Baseroot aims to become the foundational liquidity layer for decentralized knowledge, enabling sustainable AI ecosystems where contributors are fairly compensated and users receive verifiable intelligence.

### 12. Conclusion
Baseroot V2 aligns incentives between data producers, AI developers, and users. By embedding trust, transparency, and automated economics into AI workflows, the protocol establishes a new standard for knowledge monetization.
